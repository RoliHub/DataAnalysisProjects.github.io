Zenith Electronics Analysis Leveraging PostgreSQL

This section of my portfolio showcases my ability to design and implement a database management system for a business scenario. The task involved creating a database for Zenith Electronics, a leading retailer of televisions in Lagos, Nigeria. Their goal was to improve sales operations, customer service, and gain insights from their data. Database Design - I designed a relational database using PostgreSQL to store and manage Zenith's data. The database consists of four tables: sales: Stores information about each sale transaction, including customer ID, product ID, staff ID, sale date, and sale amount. customer: Stores customer details like name, email (unique identifier), phone number, and address. product: Stores product information such as brand name, model name, screen size, and price. staff: Stores staff details including name, email (unique identifier), and role (e.g., Salesperson, Manager). The tables are linked through foreign keys to ensure data integrity. For example, the sales table references the customer_id in the customer table and the product_id in the product table. The data from provided CSV files (sales.csv, customer.csv, product.csv, staff.csv) was imported into the corresponding database tables using appropriate methods like pgAdmin import tool.
